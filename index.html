What is Ascendance Foundry?

We are an AI-first talent and training partner. We embed top university students into your company as fellows. Each fellow is paired with one of your employees for a 12-week project-based fellowship. Your employee gets AI training and mentorship experience, while the fellow delivers a real project for your team.

  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)

  • Cloud AI Services (AWS, GCP, Azure)
